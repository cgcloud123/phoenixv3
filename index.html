Welcome To Our Business

Phoenix Decor Cont LLC has established itself in the UAE as one of the top leading companies in providing “complete interior solutions” since the companies inception in 2014. Specialized in fit-out works, turnkey solutions, MEP, joinery, civil, glass & aluminum works, We focus on projects that reflect our client’s personality and aspirations catering to the ever growing sophistication of the market and it’s requirements. With the in-house Phoenix Alpha Technical Services and a 25,000 sq. ft Phoenix Glass & Joinery Factory, Our Quality Management System and producitivy is taken to a whole new level. This makes us enviable and highly commendable in the market. It results in effecient management of time and money for the utmost satisfaction of our valued Clients. Over these years, our experienced team of supervisors, engineers and project managers have ensured successful execution at highest of standards. Our Clients count on us to provide complete solutions in the areas of design, engineering and planning. Our team of professionals make sure all design elements comply with the vision and needs of your business. Making us the preferred contractor of many Clients as we are committed to quality which is best exemplified by projects done right the first time.
